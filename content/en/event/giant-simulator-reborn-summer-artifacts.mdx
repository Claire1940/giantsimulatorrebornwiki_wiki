  <Step num="1" title="Set Up on the Soccer Field">

  <Step num="2" title="Activate Auto-Clicker">

    Leave your character running. This setup bypasses the standard AFK circle limits, yielding up to twice the amount of shells per minute compared to standard idle spots.
  </Step>

If you prefer active gameplay, running around the main map to collect Orbs is an excellent alternative. Orbs respawn every 60 seconds and grant a shell payout equal to your passive income tick. To maximize this, set your character size to 100 and look downward while running; your massive hitbox will vacuum up dozens of Orbs in seconds.

Once you have secured your **giant simulator reborn summer artifacts** and evolved your pet team, your focus should shift to endgame boss farming. Boss combat in Giant Simulator Reborn utilizes a strict damage cap system. No matter how high your base damage is, your hits are capped based on your total rebirth count.

| Boss Name | Location | Max Damage Cap | Rebirths Required for Max Cap | Key Rewards |
