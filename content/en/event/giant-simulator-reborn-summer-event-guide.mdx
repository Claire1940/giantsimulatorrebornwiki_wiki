<Section icon={Target} title="Shell Farming Routes & AFK Strategies">

Farming Shells is the core loop of the Summer Event. Shells are used to purchase event chests, unlock top-tier pets, and acquire powerful artifacts. There are several active and passive methods to accumulate Shells quickly, depending on your playstyle and whether you have access to a private server.

For active players, the **Mine Race Minigame** is the single fastest way to earn Shells. In this minigame, you must navigate a grid of tiles, stepping only on green tiles while avoiding red tiles. Stepping on a red tile resets your progress. Reaching the end of the race rewards you with a massive payout of 37,000+ Shells (or 74,000+ with the Event Pass).

Alternatively, active players can run around the main map to collect **Shell Orbs**. Orbs respawn every 60 seconds and provide a highly reliable shell yield that scales with your active multipliers. If you prefer passive progression, you can utilize the AFK Shell Circle or execute the highly popular Double Goal trick.

| Method | Shell Yield | Activity Level | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Mine Race Minigame** | **37,000+ Shells** per completion | Very High | Active players wanting fast bursts |
| **Double Goal AFK** | **3,000 Shells** every 5 seconds | Zero (AFK) | Private server owners with auto-clickers |
| **Map Orb Farming** | **700 - 3,000 Shells** per orb | High | Active leveling and gold farming |
| **AFK Shell Circle** | **700 - 750 Shells** every 5 seconds | Zero (AFK) | Public server players who are away |

To maximize your passive income, the Double Goal AFK trick is highly recommended. Follow these steps to set up this highly efficient farming route.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Clear the Pitch">
    Locate the soccer nets in the summer event area. Kick the middle beach ball completely out of the pitch so it does not interfere with your positioning or spawning cycles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Position Your Character">
    Stand directly between the two goalpost lines. You want to position your giant so that your weapon swings can reach both balls simultaneously.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate Auto-Clicker">
    Turn on your auto-clicker and adjust your camera angle facing downward. This ensures your swings continuously strike both beach balls into their respective nets as they spawn.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect Double Rewards">
    By scoring in both nets at once, you will receive up to 3,000 Shells every 5 seconds, making this method twice as fast as the standard AFK Shell Circle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Private Server Advantage">
The Double Goal AFK trick is highly sensitive to disruption. It is strongly recommended to use a private server (which costs 100 Robux per month) to prevent other players from moving the beach balls and ruining your AFK alignment.
</Callout>
