<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Golden Egg Quest Route">

When you receive an egg collection quest, executing a clean route saves time and keeps your gold-per-minute rates high. Follow this step-by-step route to clear your quest log quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accept the Quest">
    Visit the quest board and look for the **Collect Golden Eggs** task. If it is not available, clear lower-tier quests to cycle the board.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Check the Waterfall and Riverbed">
    Head immediately to the waterfall. One egg commonly spawns directly behind the falling water, one spawns at the bottom pool, and up to two can spawn on the ledge above the waterfall.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Scan the Trees and Sword Monument">
    Move toward the giant sword monument. Check the ground behind the sword, then scan the foliage of the nearby trees. Eggs frequently nest directly inside the leaf blocks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Check the Piers and Ledges">
    Look down the piers extending over the water. Run along the docks and jump up to the high wooden platforms where eggs spawn out of reach of smaller players.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch the Spawn Timer">
Eggs and map orbs operate on a respawn cycle. If a spot is empty, wait approximately 60 seconds for the next wave of items to populate the map.
</Callout>
