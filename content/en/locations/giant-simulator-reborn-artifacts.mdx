<Section icon={Target} title="How to Farm Quest Points & Event Currencies">

Because standard artifact boxes require Quest Points, establishing a reliable farming route is essential for mid-game progression. Quest Points are awarded by completing tasks assigned by the NPC Sir Hops a lot, located near the cave entrance. These tasks range from simple orb gathering to challenging boss battles.

To farm efficiently, you must understand the boss damage mechanics. Your damage against bosses scales directly with your total number of rebirths. Each rebirth adds +1 damage per swing to bosses, up to a strict cap. 

| Boss Name | Location | Max Damage Cap | Rebirths Needed for Cap | Key Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Borock** | Cave Arena | 30 Damage | 30 Rebirths | Gold, Quest Points, XP |
| **Demon King** | Temple Arena | 50 Damage | 50 Rebirths | Soul Gems, Quest Points |

To maximize your Quest Point yield, follow this optimized progression route:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pick Up Quests">
    Always visit Sir Hops a lot first to grab a quest before starting any farming session.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect Map Orbs">
    While waiting for bosses to spawn, shrink your character size and sweep the map to collect gold and quest-related orbs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat Bosses">
    Enter the boss portals as soon as they active. Defeating Borock provides quick gold, while the Demon King is ideal for late-game Soul Gems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Turn In and Repeat">
    Return to Sir Hops a lot immediately after completing the objectives to claim your Quest Points and XP.
  </Step>
</Steps>
