### The Size Manipulation Secret

One of the most overlooked giant simulator reborn secrets is the tactical manipulation of your character's physical size. While growing into a massive behemoth is the primary visual goal of the game, maintaining maximum size constantly is actually highly inefficient for farming. 

Experienced players frequently reduce their character size to between **12% and 15%** using the in-game size slider. This trick offers several massive advantages:
- **Precise navigation**: You can easily fit into tight spaces, such as the cave entrance, without getting stuck on geometry.
- **Rapid orb collection**: A smaller physical footprint allows you to sweep across spawning gold and event orbs much faster, clearing entire clusters in seconds.
- **Combat dodging**: Smaller hitboxes make it considerably harder for hostile players to target you in active PvP zones.

### The Pet XP Rebirth Secret

Another core secret lies in how pet experience scales with your rebirth count. For every single rebirth your character has completed, you gain **one additional pet XP per sword swing**. 

This means progression scales exponentially:

Because every single pet level increases that pet's overall stat multiplier by **5%**, rapidly leveling your companion roster through high-frequency swings becomes the single most powerful way to multiply your growth rate.
