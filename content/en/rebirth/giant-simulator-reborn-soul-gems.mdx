<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Farming: Bosses and Temple Routes">

When you are strong enough to avoid constant rebirthing, you can transition into active farming for **giant simulator reborn soul gems**. The most effective way to do this is by targeting the **Demon King**. Located in the Temple area, this boss drops a substantial amount of gems upon defeat.

However, boss farming requires a specific setup. You cannot simply walk in with starter gear; you need a combination of high-tier armor, dual-wielded weapons (thanks to the Ambidextrous skill), and pets with combat-focused stats.

| Boss Name | Location | Requirements | Gem Yield |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Borock** | Starter Zone | Low Level | Low/None |
| **Demon King** | Temple Area | High Level / Gear | **High** |
| **Boss Nest** | Event Zone | Team Required | Mixed |

**Farming Strategy:**
1. **Equip combat pets**: Look for pets that increase your Damage and Crit Chance rather than just XP.
2. **Utilize Artifacts**: Use artifacts that provide "Boss Damage" or "Soul Gem Drop" bonuses.
3. **Cycle Quests**: Always have a quest active that requires you to defeat the Demon King. This allows you to double-dip on rewards, earning Quest Points and Soul Gems simultaneously.
4. **Temple Training**: While waiting for the boss to respawn, stand in the Soul Training zones within the Temple to gain a passive trickle of gems.

<Callout type="info" title="PvP Survival">
The Temple area is often a hotspot for high-level PvP. If you are farming soul gems, consider using a private server or a low-population server to avoid being "sent back to spawn" by a larger giant while you are focused on the boss.
