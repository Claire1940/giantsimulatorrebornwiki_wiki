<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Rebirth Mechanics and Soul Gem Spending">

Rebirth is where the "Noob to Pro" transition truly happens. If you never Rebirth, you will hit a hard cap on your power. Rebirthing allows you to earn Soul Gems, which are used to purchase permanent upgrades in the Skill Tree. A pro player doesn't just Rebirth as soon as they hit Level 100; they calculate if staying a bit longer to hit a higher level will yield significantly more Soul Gems.

| Skill Name | Cost Priority | Effect | Pro Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **EXP Gain** | 1st | Increases XP from all sources | Max this first to speed up Rebirth cycles |
| **Coin Multiplier** | 2nd | Increases Gold from all sources | Essential for buying high-tier gear quickly |
| **Ambidextrous** | 3rd | Dual-wield weapons | The single biggest boost to training speed |
| **Crit Chance** | 4th | Higher damage in combat | Priority for boss hunters and PvP players |
| **Speed Gain** | 5th | Move faster across the map | Useful for questing and dodging attacks |

<Callout type="warning" title="Spending Warning">
Do not waste your first 100 Soul Gems on Speed or Crit. If you don't invest in EXP Gain or Coin Multipliers early on, your second and third Rebirths will take just as long as your first, stalling your progression.
